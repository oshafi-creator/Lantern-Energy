Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Can I switch between plans?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or switch between the renewable and legacy data packages at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What does the free trial include?

Each free trial gives you 7 days of full platform access to the core datasets in that category. No credit card required to start.

Are consulting engagements included?

Consulting is available separately or bundled at a discount for Professional+ and Institutional subscribers. Contact sales for details.

How often is the data updated?

Most datasets are updated on a 48-hour cycle. Some regulatory and government-sourced data follows their native publication schedule.

Is there an API available?

API access is available for Professional+ and Institutional plans. Custom data integrations can be configured for institutional subscribers.

Can I get a bundle discount?

Absolutely. Institutional clients frequently combine both data packages. Reach out to our sales team for custom bundle pricing.
